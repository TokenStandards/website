Finance, Meet Open Source

With Token Standards, we are working to provide secure, open source solutions for securing digital assets like Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Whether you are a private investor, financial institution, or crypto-company, storing your funds securely is of the utmost importance.

Wallet Benefits

  • Built by Professionals

    Solutions built by industry professionals experienced in building self-managed, cold generation and storage.

  • Multi-Sig Security

    Multiple keys are required to access funds; reducing custodial risk and enabling unique custom set-ups.

  • Open Source

    Audited by experts and available for public review; enabling cross-collaboration and better security.
